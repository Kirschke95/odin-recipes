Description

This is a simple beef dish that can be made in one pan in under 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion chopped like you
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Seasonin y'all

Steps

  1. Brown the meat
  2. Push meat to the side and add onion and garlic. Cook until onion is transparent.
  3. Add seasonings
  4. Enjoy
